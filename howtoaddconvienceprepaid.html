It is available at the following convenience stores.

The convenience stores mentioned above sell Nintendo prepaid cards. If you purchased a Nintendo prepaid card, scratch the scratch on the back of the card and register the Nintendo prepaid number listed on it.

At some convenience stores, you can also purchase a "Nintendo Prepaid Number" from a multimedia device. You can use the Nintendo prepaid number on the statement (receipt) you receive at the time of purchase or on the dedicated sheet. There are differences in shape and notation at each convenience store.

*The Wii U Nintendo eShop does not accept traditional Nintendo Point prepaid cards or
Nintendo Points prepaid numbers.

*Traditional Nintendo Points prepaid cards and Nintendo Points prepaid numbers
can be used on the Wii Shopping Channel and Nintendo DSi Shop.
