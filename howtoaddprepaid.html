Add balance with Nintendo prepaid number

Purchase a Nintendo prepaid card at
a Nintendo product dealer

You can use a Nintendo prepaid card to add your balance.

If you scrape the scratch part on the back of the card,
you will get a Nintendo prepaid number.

Wii U does not accept traditional Nintendo Points prepaid cards or
Nintendo Points prepaid numbers.

Legacy Nintendo Points prepaid cards and
Nintendo Points prepaid numbers can be used on the Wii Shopping Channel and
Nintendo DSi Shop.
