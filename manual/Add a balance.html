Add a balance

When you touch "Balance" in the menu bar, the Add Balance screen is displayed.

* Balances are managed for each user.

To top up your balance, you'll need one of the following:

  • Nintendo Prepaid Card
  • Nintendo Prepaid Numbers
  • credit card
  • Some prepaid e-money such as Suica and PASMO

*The added balance cannot be used on the Wii Shopping Channel.

* If you have registered the same Nintendo Network ID for your Wii U console and Nintendo 3DS series console, you can also use the charged balance on the Nintendo eShop for your Nintendo 3DS.

Use your purchased Nintendo Prepaid number to top up your balance

Select to use a Nintendo Prepaid Card or a Nintendo Prepaid Number. You can also find out how to buy Nintendo prepaid numbers.

Top up your balance with a credit card

Select if you want to use a credit card. You can check the purchase details in "Usage Record" in "My Menu". 4In
addition, if you register your credit card information, you will be able to charge your balance from the next time you just enter the password for managing your credit card information that you entered at the time of registration.

* Please note that if you enter the wrong password for credit card information management three times, your credit card information will be erased.

* If you have forgotten your password, please delete your credit card information and register again.

Verify your credit card information

You can check or delete your credit card information.

* If the user is under the age of 18, the credit card cannot be used. If you are under the age of 18 and want to use a credit card, you must have parental consent. Follow the instructions on the screen.
