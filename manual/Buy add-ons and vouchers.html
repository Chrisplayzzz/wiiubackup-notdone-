Buy add-ons and vouchers

On the software information screen 9, you can select "Additional Contents" or "Vouchers" for the software and purchase them.

Buy additional content

You can purchase additional content when it is available.

● Search for content

You can narrow down the additional content that can be purchased with the software by free words, genres, and prices.

* Depending on the additional content, it may not be possible to filter by genre.

● List of additional contents

When you touch it, the details screen of the add-on content is displayed.

● Checkbox

When you touch the add-on you want to buy, it becomes . Touch "Confirm Selected" and follow the on-screen instructions to purchase.

  • * The type and price of additional content vary depending on the software.
  • * You can check the purchase history of additional content in "Usage Records" in "My Menu". 4
  • * Purchased additional content cannot be returned, refunded, or exchanged.
  • * Once purchased, additional content can be re-downloaded for free even if it is deleted.
  • * If you delete your Nintendo Network ID, you will not be able to download it again.
  • * If the distribution is canceled or terminated, you may not be able to download it again. Thank you for your understanding.
  • * Purchased additional content will be saved in the location where the software is stored.
  • * Additional content can be used not only by the user who purchased it, but also by all users registered on the same console.

Buy a voucher

It can be purchased if there is a service available by means of a voucher.

The voucher is valid for a period of time and is valid at the time of purchase. In addition, if you purchase additional tickets before the expiration date of the purchased voucher, the number of days purchased will be added from the expiration date.

  • * The type and price of the ticket vary depending on the software.
  • * If the voucher expires, the expiration date will not be automatically extended even if there is a balance. Please purchase a voucher according to your needs.
  • * You can check the purchase history such as the purchase date (start date of use) of the voucher in "Usage Record" in "My Menu". 4
  • * Purchased tickets cannot be returned, refunded, or exchanged.
  • * Vouchers can be used not only by the user who purchased them, but also by all users registered on the same console.
  • * In order to purchase additional items while the period remains, you need to purchase them with the user who made the previous purchase.
