Enter the download number

You can enter the download number to download the software.

What is a download number?

A 16-character alphanumeric number for downloading paid or free software, additional content, or vouchers.

How do I buy a download number?

  • At Nintendo merchandise retailers and some convenience stores, you can purchase a "download card" with a download number.
  • You can buy the download number directly in the specific online shop.

* The purchase method varies depending on the site and store that handles it. For more information, please visit the Nintendo website.
