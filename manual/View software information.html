View software information

When you touch "View Details", the information screen of the software is displayed.

You can see the detailed information of the software and purchase it or add it to the list you are interested in. Also, depending on the software, you can download a trial version or purchase additional content or vouchers.

* The contents displayed vary depending on the software.

● Proceed to the purchase screen 9

● Register on the list you care about

When you touch it, it will be registered in list 4 that you are interested in.

●Trial version

You can download the trial version of the software for free.

* The number of times and time that can be started may be limited for the trial version software. Even if you delete the trial software and download it again, the number of times and the time limit will not be restored.

* The number of times and time limits are counted in total for all users registered on the console.

About "Software Evaluation"

The "Software Rating" displayed on the software information screen displays the recommendation level of everyone voted in Recommendation Poll 4 in ★increments of 0.5.

* Pre-release software is not displayed.

* For more information on the tabulation of recommended votes, please refer to "How to Count Votes".
