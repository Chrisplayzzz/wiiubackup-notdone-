Watch the video

If you select a video on the software information screen, you can enjoy the video related to the software.

1

2

3

4

1Pause/Play button

You can pause it by touching it. Press it again to play.

2Playbar

Represents the playback position. to move back or forward in the playback position.

3Screen Fit Button

The image can be displayed on the entire height or width of the TV screen. Touch it again to go back.

4option

The title of the video and related videos are displayed. You can also change the quality of the video and touch "Software Information" to move to the software information screen.

  • * Depending on the communication environment you are using, the video and audio may be interrupted.
  • * Videos cannot be saved to the main unit storage memory, USB storage media, or SD card.

If you want to operate with buttons or sticks

The video can also be controlled with the buttons and sticks on the Wii U GamePad.
