What is Nintendo eShop?

The Nintendo eShop is an online store where you can purchase Wii U download software and additional content. You can also watch detailed information about the software, watch an introductory video, and download a trial version.

  • * To purchase software, etc., you need to charge (deposit) your balance. 8
  • * If you register the same Nintendo Network ID on the Wii U console and the Nintendo 3DS series system, you can share the balance and usage records with that ID.

Virtual Console Preferential Pricing Announcement

If you already own a Wii version of a Virtual Console title (such as a Virtual Console title purchased on the Wii and moved to the Wii U, or a Virtual Console title purchased from the Wii Shopping Channel in the Wii menu), you will be eligible for a preferential price when purchasing the Wii U version of Virtual Console with the same title.
